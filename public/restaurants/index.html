Our Story

Eco Eats started off as a small local convenience store in Ballard called Ready-Mart. The owner, Ricky Solomon, had a life changing heart attack in 2017 that caused him to reevaluate his eating habits. Having made many dietary changes, Ricky realized the importance of a healthy diet. He remodeled his store into Eco Eats and replaced his products with fresh and organic foods. Now he is the proud owner of a healthy market that has expanded into Queen Anne and the University District. Customers can easily place orders online for pickup or delivery with the choice of doing bike delivery to be more eco-friendly! Feast easily and healthily at Eco Eats!
